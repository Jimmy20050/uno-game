How to Play UNO

🎯 Objective

Be the first to play all your cards!

🃏 Card Types

  • Number Cards (0-9): Match by number or color
  • Skip (🚫): Skip the next player's turn
  • Reverse (🔄): Reverse the direction of play
  • Draw Two (+2): Next player draws 2 cards
  • Wild (🌈): Play anytime, choose the color
  • Wild Draw Four (+4): Choose color, next player draws 4

🎮 Gameplay

  • Match the top card by color, number, or symbol
  • Draw a card if you can't play
  • Shout "UNO!" when you have one card left
  • First to empty their hand wins!
